Peanutbutter & Jelly Sandwich






About

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich (PB&J) consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread. The sandwich may be open-faced, made of a single slice of bread folded over, or made between two slices of bread. The sandwich is popular in the United States, especially among children

What you'll need

Two slices of bread, Peanut butter, Jelly, a bread and butter knife, one paper towel, and a plate.

Allergies

Gluten, Peanuts, Grape/Strawberry (depending on jelly), & wheat

Directions

Put the bread on the plate, spread the peanut butter on the first slice of bread, then lick off the butter knife , then use the knife and spread jelly on the other slice, put the bread slices together, cut the sandwich in half with a bread knife, and enjoy!
